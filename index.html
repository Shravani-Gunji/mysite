EXTINCTION OF ANIMALS AND STEPS TO SAVE IT

Extinctions happen when a species dies out from cataclysmic events, evolutionary problems, or human interference.There are five major causes of extinction: habitat loss, an introduced species, pollution, population growth, and overconsumption.
The steps to save extinction of animals are:

  1. Buy Eco-Friendly Products
  2. Follow The 3-R Rule: Recycle, Reuse, Reduce
  3. Do not Buy Souvenirs Made From Endangered Species
  4. Eat Less Meat
  5. Spread Awareness: get involved
